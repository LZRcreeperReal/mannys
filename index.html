The Shop

Been here a while.
Still the best one.

Manny's isn't trying to be anything fancy. It's a barbershop — chairs, clippers, and guys who actually know what they're doing. People come in, get a good cut, and come back two weeks later. That's it.

"I drive past two other shops to get here. The fade is always clean, the wait is never bad, and they don't make you feel like you're inconveniencing them."

We do fades, tapers, scissor cuts, beard lineups, straight razor shaves, and kids cuts. The kids thing matters — half our regulars started coming here because someone trusted us with a nervous 4-year-old and it went well. We're patient. We're not in a rush.

Walk-ins are fine. Bring the whole family. Tell us what you want and we'll handle it. If you don't know what you want, we'll figure it out together. No judgment, no upselling, no attitude.

This is a neighborhood shop. One of the last real ones in this part of Long Island. You'll notice the difference when you sit down.
